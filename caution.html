Caution Icon

Each alert notification displayed at the top of the weather view page has an associated caution icon. Like alert icons, caution icons are of three categories:

  1. Advisory or statement.
  2. Watch
  3. Warning

Only the most recent caution icon will be shown for any forecast day. In the case where several alert notifications are posted in the forecast for that day, a red badge icon with a count will be displayed next to the caution icon. Whenever a caution icon is shown in the weather forecast, tapping on the icon displays further details of the alert notification. Swiping on the alert detail view will reveal another alert detail if the badge count is greater than one.
